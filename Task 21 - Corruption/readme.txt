In order to combat the shadow economy, the bank decided to implement the integration of the N accounts of the company into one. In one operation, 2 accounts are combined 
and the bank automatically transfers to its account P% of the amount of the union for performing the operation and closing one of the accounts. What is the largest amount 
that can remain on the company account? On each of the accounts before the introduction of the merger policy was no more than G UAH.

Input
The first line contains 2 numbers: the number of accounts N and the percentage of deductions P.
The second line contains N numbers: the amount on each of the accounts of the company.

Output
The largest amount that can remain on the account.
